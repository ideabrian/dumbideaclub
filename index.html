Who says it's dumb?

Dumb Ideas -> Innovation

Need to finish an idea...

It's too easy to fall prey to "this is a dumb idea" -- when you start something and see it get hard. Or, maybe you just see somebody doing it great already - and you're afraid to compete. Doesn't matter. The Dumb Idea Club is a place to start quickly, make something small, and get results you can be proud of. Sure, it might be dumb, but it's hard to be consistently dumb while you're producing great stuff for the world to use.

Don't Go Alone

Every idea is a fork that leads to 12 more ideas. Some good, some proven - but, let's face it - some just won't work. The dumb ideas are points of departure; entertainment; novel brain connections. Keep them as part of your toolset and let others help you refine your thinking.

The 80's

If dumb ideas were given words and a synthesizer backup, the 80's were the birth place. Imagine what it would be like to have your own one-hit wonder? What if it was just an idea that became an earworm that people can't get rid of. It doesn't have to make sense. But it could make money. Let the 80's big hair, neon colors and fantastic music inspire you to embrace the "dumb" in your ideas.
